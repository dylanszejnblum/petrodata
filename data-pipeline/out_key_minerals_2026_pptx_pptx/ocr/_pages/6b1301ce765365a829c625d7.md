## Uses in the energy transition

- Batteries for electric vehicles
- Nickel alloys in power generation equipment
- Stainless steel pipes for CO2 capture and storage

None

## Resources in Argentina

&lt; 10,000 Tonnes

Las Águilas, Fiambalá, Sierra del Espinal, Alkaline Magmatism in the Cordillera Oriental and Sub-Andean Ranges, Salamanca District
